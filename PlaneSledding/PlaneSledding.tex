\begin{question} \label{Qdfdsfenmdr3r3}

A small mass is released from rest at the point $A$ with coordinates $(1,3,2)$. The mass slides under the influence of gravity along the plane through $A$ normal to the vector ${\bf n}= \langle 1, -1, 4\rangle$. The force of gravity points in the direction of the negative $z$-axis.
