\begin{question} \label{Qfer834443}

Let $O$ be the origin in $\mathbb{R^3}$. 

Given

\begin{enumerate}
\item a point $A$ and a vector ${\bf v}$,

\item a point $B$ and a positive scalar $b$,
\end{enumerate}

\begin{enumerate}

\item find an expression for the position(s) (relative to the orign) of the point(s) where the line through $A$ parallel to ${\bf v}$ intersects the sphere of radius $b$ centered at $B$. Your expression should be in terms of the scalar $b$ and the vectors $\overrightarrow{OA}$, $\overrightarrow{OB}$, ${\bf v}$.

\item Use the worksheet below to check your work. 

\end{enumerate}
