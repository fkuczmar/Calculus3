\begin{question}  \label{Qgtyhtsry}
(a) Find quadratic approximations to the component functions of the space curve
\[
   {\bf p}(t) = \langle  3 \cos t, 3\sin t, e^t \rangle , -2\leq t \leq 2
\]
to find the quadratic approximation to the curve at the point $(3,0,1)$. Write the approximation as a linear combination of the vectors ${\bf p}^\prime(0)$ and ${\bf p}^{\prime\prime}(0)$.

\end{question}
