\begin{question} \label{Qfer834443}

Let $O$ be the origin in $\mathbb{R^3}$. 

Given non-colinear points $A,B,C \in \mathbb{R}^3$, parameterize each of the following line segments.

\begin{enumerate}

\item the sides of $\Delta ABC$.

\item the angle bisectors of $\Delta ABC$. These are the segments that bisect the angles of the triangle and run from each vertex to its opposite side.

\item Use the worksheet below to check your work by entering the parameterizations in the worksheet.

\end{enumerate}
